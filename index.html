Global Chat Custom Rank

Buying a custom rank allows you to choose your own image and name for the rank that gets displayed in Global Chat when you talk there. Before you go ahead and purchase one, however, there are a few things you should know.

  • The image file you choose must be under 256kb in size
  • The image must have a 1:1 aspect ratio
  • The rank title can be up to 32 characters long, and supports a-z, A-Z, 1-9, and underscores
  • The image's content as well as the rank title must not violate any Global Chat rules
  • The image and rank title cannot be a copy or imitation of any existing official roles

If that sounds doable, you can fill out the form below to get started. Once you've made the payment, message @cheesits#0456 on Discord to complete the process. If you are unable to direct message the account due to not having any mutual servers, join HaileyBot's support server at https://server.haileybot.com, then try again.
